Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Take-a-Break 
    (Referred to below as "The Application") for iOS and MacOS.

Effective Date: August 17, 2020


Information Collection, Use and Sharing:

The Application does not collect, store or share any personal information.

The Application stores only data relevant to its core functionality. 
That data does not leave your device (phone, tablet or computer) unless you explicitly share it.

When you open The Application you might be asked to allow Notifications.
This is part of the core functionality and The Application uses notifications to inform you
when a time interval or a time pattern has ended. The Application will never send you a notification that is not related to this functionality.
